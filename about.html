About the pet stop

The Pet Stop is a great place to relax and entertain yourself with the lovely pets we have. You are also welcome to bring your cherished pets here.

Pet foods are available here. We also have a playground for both you and your dearest pet. If you want an alone time with your pet, we also have special rooms available.

Come now and visit us at 123 DPS Compound, Baguio City, Philippines 2600. For more information you may contact us at 074-487-112 or email us at thepetstop@petstop.com
